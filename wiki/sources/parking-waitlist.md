## Key Takeaways
- When all parking slots are booked, employees can join a **waitlist** (IRCTC/train-style queue).
- Works on **First-Come-First-Serve** at the level (floor) level — not global.
- Employees see their real-time **waitlist position number** (W/L #3, W/L #72) to gauge likelihood of getting a slot.
- **Flexible waitlist joining**: employee can join a single level's waitlist or multiple levels' waitlists simultaneously to improve chances.
- Employees can still independently book a slot at a different level even while on a waitlist for another level.
- Slot opens up when an existing booking is cancelled or user doesn't check in (auto-release).
